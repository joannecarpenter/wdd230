Membership Levels

Benefits

NP Membership

The NP Membership is for non-profit organizations. There is no fee associated with this level of membership. It includes all of the benefits of our Bronze Membership.

Bronze Membership

The Bronze Membership is our most basic level of membership with an annual fee of just $300. It includes access to our weekly digital newsletter, access to our current membership directory, and a business advertisement spot in our visitor's guide.

Silver Membership

The Silver Membership is one step above the basic membership with an annual fee of $350. It includes all of the benefits of the Bronze Membership, plus a quarterly business spotlight on our website and a reserved space at all chamber-hosted events.

Gold Membership

The Gold Membership is our highest level of membership with an annual fee of $400. It includes all of the benefits of the Silver Membership, plus an all-access pass to all chamber-hosted events, priority registration for community events, and a seat on the chamber outreach panel.
